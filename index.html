Your World, Your Projects

Since 1998, Hooks & Crafts has provided high-quality, affordable hooks and yarn to meet every crochet-related need. Our cozy shop is located in the heart of Aylmer, just a short drive from Ottawa. Our mission is to provide clients of all skill levels with a safe space to learn the art of crochet and polish their skills. Furthermore, we believe the finest materials will always lead to outstanding projects. Join us, and let's grow the crochet community together!
